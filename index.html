About the app:


This web application recreates the "Prey-predator" model: numerically (using Euler's method) solves the Lotka-Volterra equations – a system of ordinary differential equations – and builds a graph of the dependence of the number of predators and prey on time and a graph of the dependence of the number of prey on the number of predators.


About model:


The predator-prey model is a common tool that researchers develop continuously to predict the dynamics of the animal population within a certain phenomenon. For this model we make some assumptions: the predator species is totally dependent on a single prey species as its only food supply, the prey species has an unlimited food supply, there is no threat to the prey other than the specific predator.


How to use:


In order to use this application, it is necessary to enter parameters in the sections “initial predators”, “initial prey”, “prey birthrate”, “prey mortality rate”, “predator mortality rate”, “predator birthrate” and click on the "Submit" button. After that, one of the graphs will be drawn. To display the graph of the dependence of the number of predators and prey on time, press "Graph 1" (it is the default graph); to display the graph of the dependence of the number of prey on the number of predators, press "Graph 2".
